Google Playstore

Google Play, previously Android Market, is a Google-operated and established digital distribution company. It acts as the Android operating system's official app store, allowing users to search and access applications created using the Android Software Development Kit (SDK) and released by Google. Google Play is also a digital media platform, selling songs, books, videos, and TV programmes.

Apps are available at no charge or at a cost from Google Play. These can be downloaded directly from an Android device via the mobile app in the Play Store, or installed from the Google Play website to a computer. Applications which exploit a device's hardware capabilities can be targeted at device users with different hardware components.
